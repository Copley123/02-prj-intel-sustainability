Intel’s Sustainability Timeline

Explore Intel’s current and future sustainability milestones. Our company is not only dedicated to technology but to the environment as well.

2020

100% Green Power in U.S. and EU

Intel achieved 100% renewable energy use in its U.S. and European operations.

2022

Net Positive Water

Intel restored more water than it consumed in its global operations.

2025

Reduce Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Intel aims to reduce its greenhouse gas emissions by 10% from 2020 levels.

2027

Increase Energy Efficiency

Intel plans to improve the energy efficiency of its products by 30%.

2028

Zero Waste to Landfill (Progress)

Intel continues progress toward sending zero waste to landfill from global manufacturing operations.

2030

Zero Waste to Landfill

Goal: Send zero waste to landfill from Intel’s global manufacturing operations.

2032

100% Renewable Electricity Worldwide

Intel aims to use 100% renewable electricity in all global operations.

2035

Water Positive Operations

Intel plans to restore more water than it uses in all regions.

2040

Net-Zero Greenhouse Gas Emissions

Goal: Achieve net-zero greenhouse gas emissions in global operations.
